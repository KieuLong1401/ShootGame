Basic Idea:
    - Web platform's online shooting tank game.

Features:
    - Play online.
    - Player based camera.
    - Mini map.
    - Kill leader board.
    - Kill announce.
    - Tank:
        + Movement.
        + Shoot.
        + Die.
        + Kill.
        + Limit bullet range.
        + Delay shot.
        + Get random color.
        + Heal.
    - Hexagon grid background.
    - Map limit.
    - Mobile version.
